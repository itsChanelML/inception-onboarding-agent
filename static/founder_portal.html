Section 3
Your Technical Architecture

Aria’s recommended stack for ClaraVision — plain language alongside technical specs so you and your team can read this together.

The Gray Spine — Universal infrastructure every Inception founder runs on
GPU Compute → CUDA / cuDNN → NIM → API Gateway → On-Premise Hospital Deploy
NVIDIA Clara
A complete AI platform built specifically for medical imaging — an AI toolkit designed by people who understand how hospitals work.
For ClaraVision, Clara gives you pre-built medical imaging models you can fine-tune on your own data instead of building from scratch — cutting development time significantly.
Technical: NVIDIA Clara Imaging, MONAI framework, federated learning support.
NIM Microservices — On-Premise
Run your AI model inside the hospital’s own walls — patient data never travels to an outside server.
For ClaraVision, this is your compliance architecture. Patient data stays inside the hospital by design — that distinction matters enormously to procurement teams.
Technical: Self-hosted NIM containers. No data egress. HIPAA-compliant by architecture.
MONAI — Medical Open Network for AI
An open-source framework built specifically for medical imaging AI — it speaks the clinical language your model needs.
MONAI gives your engineering team a starting point that already understands medical imaging data formats, annotation standards, and clinical validation requirements.
Technical: MONAI for training, validation, deployment. Integrates with NVIDIA Clara and NIM.
Federated Learning — NVIDIA FLARE
Train your model across multiple hospitals without any hospital ever sharing patient data with another.
Your model gets smarter with every hospital that joins — but no hospital ever sees another’s data. That’s the feature that turns your first partnership into a network.
Technical: NVIDIA FLARE. Multi-site training with privacy preservation. Scales horizontally.
DGX Cloud on Google Cloud
The most powerful AI computing infrastructure available — without buying any hardware.
Train and refine your model during development on DGX Cloud. When you’re ready for hospital deployment, move to on-premise NIM. While building, DGX Cloud gives you H100 compute to iterate fast.
Technical: DGX Cloud A3 VMs, H100 GPUs on GCP. 30% Inception discount via NVIDIA × Google Cloud.
